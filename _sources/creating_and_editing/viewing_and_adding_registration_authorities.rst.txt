How to View and Add Registration Authorities
=============================================

.. note:: This can only be done if you are an Administrator

1. To get started, click on Administrator Tools at the bottom of the left menu

.. screenshot:: 
   :server_path: /account/home
   :alt: 
   :login: {'url': '/login', "username": "alice", "password": "Administrator"}
   :clicker: #dashboard-nav a[href='/account/admin']
   :browser_height: 733

2. Click the Add Registration Authority link

.. screenshot:: 
   :server_path: /account/admin
   :alt: 
   :clicker: a[href="/registrationauthority/create"] 
   :browser_height: 200
   
3. Type in the name of the new Registration Authority and a definition for the RA

.. screenshot:: 
   :server_path: /registrationauthority/create
   :alt: 
   :clicker: 
   :browser_height: 733
