How to Add a Workgroup¶

Note

You have to be an Administrator to add a workgroup

  1. Go to “Administrator Tools” at the bottom of the Dashboard side menu
  1. Select “add workgroup”

3. You will then be asked to give the new workgroup a name, which helps members identify the workgroups they are in, and then the definition of the workgroup, which let’s people know what the role of the workgroup is

  1. Then select the users you want in a particular workgroup, by assigning them to the roles they will perform:

5. Once done, click “save and continue editing” or “save” to finish creating your new workgroup, you will then recieve a message at the top of your screen saying that you have sucessfully created a new workgroup
